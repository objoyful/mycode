Here are the Python String Escape Sequences.

Escape - Description
---------------------
\\ - Backslash
\' - Single-quote
\" - Double-quote
\a - Bell
\b - Backspace
\f - Formfeed
\n - Newline
\r - Carriage
\t - Tab
\v - Vertical Tab
